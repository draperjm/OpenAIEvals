The evaluation method described in pull request 719 tests the ability of GPT-3.5-Turbo to correctly answer Japanese driving license questions. The dataset used for evaluation is based on Q&A published for students to prepare for the real exam by Japanese driving school. The evaluation assesses the model's ability to understand high-context sentences with ambiguous meanings in short sentences. The evaluation JSON data includes at least 5 evaluation samples with input and ideal output. The model was able to correctly answer only 26 out of 60 questions (43.33%).
