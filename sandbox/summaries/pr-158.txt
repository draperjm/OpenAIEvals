The evaluation method described in pull request 158 is called "cube-pack" and tests the physical reasoning of GPT by asking how many solid cubes can be fit into a larger box. The problems are designed to be very easy, so that a human can easily perform the task with 100% accuracy. The evaluation has an exact answer, is easy for a human, and is clearly aligned with the reasoning of the physical world. The evaluation has an accuracy of 0.02 with 109 tests.
