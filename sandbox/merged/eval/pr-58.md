### Eval name

decrypt-caesar-cipher

### Eval description

Instruct GPT to decrypt a cipher, given a 'shift' int between 1 and 5. Chunks of up to 100 characters from open
literature.

### What makes this a useful eval?

Language models have notoriously not performed well with decryption and general string manipulation. ChatGPT struggles
to perform at shift=1, even for shorter text.
