The evaluation method described in pull request 729 aims to assess the ability of a GPT-3.5-Turbo model to accurately recognize information in the titles of typical Japanese caregiving job listings. The evaluation involves multiple-choice questions with answer choices that vary in the amount of information provided, including job type, qualifications, and employment status. The correct answer rate was 60%, indicating that the task is highly challenging. The evaluation is considered useful because accurately handling job information is important for nearly all humans who come into contact with job postings, including millions of people involved in Japanese caregiving jobs. The evaluation includes JSON data with at least 5 evaluation samples.
