Pull request 589 proposes an evaluation method called Emotional Intelligence, which assesses GPT's ability to understand and manage emotional situations using modified versions of well-validated tests. The evaluation measures the AI's ability to understand and analyze emotional information, not its emotional intelligence in the human sense. The evaluation is useful for identifying areas for improvement and better understanding the limitations of GPT in handling emotional contexts, which is important for AI applications that involve interactions with humans. The evaluation provides a unique value by testing GPT's capacity to understand and manage emotions, contributing to the ongoing development of AI models that are more attuned to human emotions and can provide better support in emotionally charged situations. The performance of GPT-4 in this evaluation was an accuracy of 0.48.
