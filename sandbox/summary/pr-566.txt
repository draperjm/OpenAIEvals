Pull request 566 proposes an evaluation method for GPT-3.5-Turbo related to financial math. The evaluation consists of asking the model to calculate the amount of interest owed on a credit card by a certain date, given a specific scenario. The purpose of this evaluation is to test the model's ability to combine math with its understanding of finance, which is a crucial area where LLMs can be useful. The evaluation includes several scenarios with different inputs and expected outputs, provided in JSON format. The goal is to ensure that the model can natively understand financial concepts and apply math to them, which is essential for reliable integration into comparison products.
